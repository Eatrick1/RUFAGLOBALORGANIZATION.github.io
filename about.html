Orphan Support Programs

Comprehensive care and support for orphaned children to help them build stable futures

Safe Shelter Program

Providing safe, nurturing homes for orphans with 24/7 caregiver support and family-style living environment.

  • Safe housing with beds
  • Nutritious meals daily
  • Clean clothing & hygiene
  • 24/7 caregiver support

Education Support

Ensuring orphans receive quality education from primary through secondary and vocational training.

  • School fees paid in full
  • Uniforms & textbooks
  • After-school tutoring
  • Career guidance

Healthcare & Counseling

Comprehensive medical care and psychological support to help orphans heal and thrive.

  • Regular medical check-ups
  • Psychological counseling
  • Trauma therapy
  • Life skills training
