Frequently Asked Questions

How can I donate to RUFA Global Organization?

You can donate to RUFA Global through several methods:

  • Online: Use our secure donation form on the Donate page
  • Bank Transfer: Send funds to our bank account (details available upon request)
  • Mobile Money: Use +256 772 342831 for mobile money donations
  • In-Person: Visit our office to make a donation directly

All donations are tax-deductible and you will receive a receipt for your records.

How can I volunteer with your organization?

We welcome volunteers in various capacities:

  • Field Volunteers: Assist with our programs in communities across Uganda
  • Office Volunteers: Help with administrative tasks at our headquarters
  • Skill-Based Volunteers: Offer professional skills (medical, educational, technical)
  • International Volunteers: We accept international volunteers for periods of 1-12 months

To apply, please fill out our volunteer application form which you can request by emailing volunteer@rufaglobal.org.

What areas of Uganda do you work in?

RUFA Global operates in multiple regions across Uganda, with a focus on underserved rural communities. Our current active regions include:

  • Central Region: Kampala, Wakiso, Mukono
  • Eastern Region: Karamoja, Kaberamaido, Kihihi
  • Western Region: Bukomansimbi, Mbarara
  • Northern Region: Gulu, Lira

We are continuously expanding our reach to serve more communities in need across the country.

How can my organization partner with RUFA Global?

We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our vision. Partnership opportunities include:

  • Corporate Partnerships: Employee engagement, CSR initiatives, cause marketing
  • NGO Collaborations: Joint programming, resource sharing, capacity building
  • Government Partnerships: Policy advocacy, program implementation support
  • Academic Partnerships: Research collaborations, internship programs

For partnership inquiries, please email partnerships@rufaglobal.org with details about your organization and proposed collaboration.

How can I get updates about your work?

Stay connected with our work through multiple channels:

  • Newsletter: Subscribe to our monthly newsletter for updates
  • Social Media: Follow us on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn
  • Annual Report: Download our annual report from our website
  • Events: Attend our fundraising and awareness events

You can also visit our office to learn more about our programs and impact firsthand.

Are my donations tax-deductible?

Yes, RUFA Global Organization is a registered non-profit organization in Uganda, and donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

We provide official donation receipts for all contributions, which can be used for tax purposes. For international donors, we recommend consulting with a tax professional in your country regarding the deductibility of donations to foreign charities.

For specific questions about tax deductions, please contact finance@rufaglobal.org.
