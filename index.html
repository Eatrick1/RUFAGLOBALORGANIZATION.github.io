Social Activists From All Regions

We feed and give to homeless children and underprivileged children from different communities, both in and around Kampala and different parts of the country. In 2025, we fed 2,300 children from these communities.

95+
House Holds (2025)

Children (2021-2025)

In January 2025, in partnership with Gifted Hands Africa and Rissa Naturals, we distributed food to over 50 families in Sissa-Buzzi and over 60 families in Kasanvu, Namuwongo. Each family received 5 kgs of rice, maize flour, sugar and beans, along with soap and masks.

We raised over 1,500 plates of food during our 2024 Christmas Drive, which we delivered to the children we support in Sissa Buzzi, The Differently Abled Children's home in Nampunge, The Ray of Hope Children's Home in Namuwongo and street children from around Nakivubo, Owino and Kisenyi.
